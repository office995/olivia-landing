Everything you need to know.

How is this different from ChatGPT? +
ChatGPT answers questions. Olivia runs your business. She is connected to your real data — your Gmail, your calendar, your finances, your CRM. She acts on your behalf, not just responds to prompts. And she remembers everything, building a deeper model of your business every day.
What tools does it connect to? +
Gmail, Google Calendar, Notion, GoHighLevel, Stripe, Telegram, Google Sheets, and more integrations are added continuously. The Professional and Agency tiers support unlimited integrations including custom API connections.
How long does setup take? +
Under 10 minutes. We walk you through every step with a guided onboarding flow. Connect your first three tools, tell Olivia about your business, and your first morning briefing arrives tomorrow at 7am.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. No contracts. No cancellation fees. Cancel in one click from your settings page. We're confident you won't want to — but you're never locked in.
Is my data safe? +
Your business data never leaves your own integrations. Olivia reads from your connected tools to answer questions and generate briefings, but she does not store your private emails, financial data, or messages on our servers. You own your data entirely.

A unique feature that lets you channel the thinking of 23 of history's greatest minds — Jeff Bezos, Robert Greene, Naval Ravikant, Marcus Aurelius, Charlie Munger, and more. Ask any business question and hear how each of them would approach it. No other product on the market offers anything like it.
